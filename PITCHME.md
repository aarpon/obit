# openBIS Importer Toolset (oBIT)

---

## Flow Cytometry

### Supported hardware

* BD LSRFortessa
* BD FACS Aria III
* BD Influx
* Bio-Rad S3e
* BC MoFlo XDP
* *Sony SH800* (planned)

---

## Microscopy

### Supported hardware

* All single-file datasets supported by the bio-formats library
* Composite datasets:
    - YouScope 
    - Visitron ND (STK or TIFF)
    - Leica TIFF series
    - Generic TIFF series
